What does HestaBit do?

HestaBit Technologies is a next-generation IT consulting and product engineering company helping enterprises, startups, and governments harness the power of AI, enterprise platforms, and emerging technologies to achieve measurable business outcomes.

When was HestaBit founded?

Hestabit was founded in 2012. The company's founders include Dipanshu Upadhyay, Prashant Gautam, and Harshvardhan Lakhera.

Where is HestaBit located?

Hestabit's main headquarters is in Noida, India, specifically in Sector 63, D-Block, at address D-247/32. The company also has international offices in London, UK; Houston, USA; and Dubai.
